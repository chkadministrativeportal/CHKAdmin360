Online Forms

This page includes automated submission portals for commonly requested services under the CHK Administrative Section. Please note that most of these forms are for CHK faculty and staff only. If these forms do not apply to you, please do not submit as your requests will not be processed.

Work Order

Use this form to request facility repairs. This includes sanitation, electrical work, plumbing, or carpentry. Please provide a detailed description of the issue and the specific location.

Submit a Work Order / Job Request

Fund Request

Use this form to request reimbursement or a cash advance for small, urgent expenses (upon approval of the College AO and Dean). Please take note that this form is for CHK faculty and staff only.

Request a Fund/Petty

Meal Request

Use this form to request food and beverage for official meetings, seminars, or any authorized CHK events. Requests should be submitted at least 3 weeks in advance and must include the final head count.

Request for Snacks/Meal Provision

CHK RAP

Use this form to notify the CHK Administrative Section of any facility issues, safety hazards, or equipment malfunctions specific to the CHK Sports Complex.

Report A Problem

PPMP Online Form

Use this digital form to submit your unit's annual procurement requirements. This enables the Supply Officer to consolidate all requested supplies, equipment, and services for the upcoming fiscal year.

Submit Item Proposal

COS Wellness Leave

Use this form to notify the CHK Administrative Section of wellness leave requests. This application is applicable only to Contract of Service (COS) staff.

File Leave

A.I.R.A. System

Admin Inventory Request Application

A.I.R.A., or the CHK Admin Inventory Request Application, is strictly for use by CHK faculty and staff only. Any unauthorized requests will not be entertained. The purpose of AIRA is to streamline and automate all supply requests and to collect accurate data on the most frequently requested supplies. This data will be used to support planning and preparation for the college’s APP-CSE procurement requests, ensuring that needed supplies are identified efficiently and in a timely manner.

REMINDER: Please request only what is necessary and avoid stockpiling supplies. Usage patterns will be monitored to help ensure responsible and efficient use of resources. The Admin Section may review and adjust requests when warranted. .
